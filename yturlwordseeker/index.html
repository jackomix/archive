about

based off vsauce's what is random video, this tool allows you to easily search for text in a YouTube video's url (which are made up of random characters). this page was featured in CHM Tech's YouTube easter eggs, which is why this page was updated.

note: google's ai is bad at stuff like this, so you'll get a lot of unrelevant pages. I've tried to look for ways to get past some of them but I haven't been successful. feel free to reach out to me if you find a solution though.
